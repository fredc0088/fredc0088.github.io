Gemini's Recommendation

If you want the most "Academic" features with the least amount of manual work, al-folio is hard to beat. It handles citations and publication lists more gracefully than almost any other theme. However, if you prefer a more "Classic CV" sidebar look, Academic Pages is the way to go.
