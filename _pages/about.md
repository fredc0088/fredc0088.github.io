I am a London-based Senior Software Engineer with over seven years of experience architecting high-performance backend systems for industry leaders like **The Walt Disney Company**, **Sainsbury’s**, and the **BBC**. My core expertise is in the **Scala/JVM ecosystem**, where I specialize in building reactive, event-driven microservices using purely functional stacks (**Cats Effect, ZIO, FS2**) and the **Akka** ecosystem.

Throughout my career, I have focused on solving complex distributed systems challenges—from leading OIDC Provider migrations at Disney to scaling metadata delivery for millions of users at BBC Sounds. I am particularly adept at leveraging **AWS infrastructure** (Lambda, Kinesis, DynamoDB) and **Kafka** to create resilient, real-time data pipelines.

### Technical Deep Dive

- **Languages:** Scala (Expert), Java, F#, Python, PowerShell.
- **Ecosystems:** Pure Functional Programming (Cats, ZIO), Akka (Actors, Streams, Sharding), Type-safe APIs (Http4s, Tapir).
- **Data & Streaming:** Kafka, Spark, SQL (PostgreSQL), Doobie/Slick.
- **Cloud:** Advanced AWS Architecture, Terraform/IaC, Kubernetes/Docker.

### Personal Interests & Research

Beyond professional backend engineering, I am a passionate advocate for the intersection of **Functional Programming** and **Data Science**. I have a strong academic and personal interest in **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and **Text Mining**—exemplified by my first-class honors project, an _Intelligent Documents Organiser Platform_ built in Scala.

Currently, I am exploring the potential of **Distributed Ledger Technologies (Blockchain)** and the application of **Machine Learning** to elevate professional automation and code design. I thrive on continuous improvement and am always looking to bridge the gap between rigorous engineering and emerging AI technologies.
